# Notable Quotes

## 2026-06-04 — On anthropomorphic language for LLMs — Thomas Doe (ACM webinar)

**Original (as spoken):**

> I am taken aback every time anew when people refer to LLMs and agents "deciding", "looking" or otherwise referred to with verbs or attributes that are humans. LLMs PROCESS, READ, WRITE, CALCULATE. They do not "decide" or "look".... as ACM we should have some obligation to infuse proper language into how people talk.

**Polished:**

> I am taken aback anew each time I hear people describe LLMs and agents as "deciding", "looking", or otherwise ascribe to them verbs and attributes that belong to humans. LLMs PROCESS, READ, WRITE, CALCULATE. They do not "decide" or "look".... As ACM, we should bear some obligation to instill precise language into how people talk about these systems.
